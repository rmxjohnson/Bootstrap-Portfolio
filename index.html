About Me

I currently work as a Software Quality Assurance Analyst for Foundation Software, Inc., in Strongsville, Ohio. In testing the software at my workplace, I became interested in learning how to develop the code that I test on a daily basis. I first began my college career at Case Western Reserve University and am now excited to be completing the Full-Stack Web Development bootcamp through CWRU.


For recreation, I enjoy a variety of activities. In warm weather, I prefer being outdoors. I enjoy hiking, kayaking, biking, and jogging. Last spring, I finished my 1st half-marathon. Not a stellar finish-time, but was satisfied to complete the race. I like working in my flowers outside but do not have much of a green thumb. I love traveling and hope to plan a trip overseas in the near future. I also like reading and belong to a bookclub with coworkers. Over the past winter, I have completed several projects around the house, becoming quite proficient at painting. I like the sense of accomplishment I feel when they have been completed.
